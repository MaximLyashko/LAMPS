==Instructions==
Cut one of every part using 1/4" clear acrylic, except for vat_side.svg... you need two of those.

===Parts===
Vat Bottom (vat_bottom.svg) - Qty 1
Vat Side   (vat_side.svg)   - Qty 2
Vat Back   (vat_back.svg)   - Qty 1
Vat Front  (vat_front.svg)  - Qty 1
Vat Handle (vat_handle.svg) - Qty 1

*Glue all parts together, clamping as necessary.

*Once parts are glued together and vat has dried, use silicon to create a water tight seal on vat inside edges.

*You'll need to apply something (unkown) to the vat floor to prevent cured acrylic from sticking.
